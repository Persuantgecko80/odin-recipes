Ingredients

  • Celestial carrots - chopped or diced
  • Nebula celery - chopped or diced
  • Cosmic onions - finely chopped
  • Alien bell peppers - chopped or diced
  • Zucchini from distant galaxies - chopped or diced
  • Stardust potatoes - diced
  • Hyperspace garlic cloves - minced
  • Interstellar vegetable broth - 4 cups
  • Canned diced tomatoes from celestial gardens - 1 can
  • Tomato paste from celestial vines - 2 tablespoons
  • Nebular herbs and spices - 1 teaspoon or to taste
  • Stellar salt - to taste
  • Black pepper from the outer cosmos - to taste
  • Nebulaic olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Cosmic garnish of intergalactic herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare for a cosmic adventure by gathering the celestial ingredients.
  2. In a stellar pot, saut√© celestial carrots, nebula celery, cosmic onions, alien bell peppers, zucchini from distant galaxies, and stardust potatoes.
  3. Add minced hyperspace garlic cloves and stir until their aromas permeate the interstellar mixture.
  4. Pour in interstellar vegetable broth, celestial diced tomatoes, and tomato paste extracted from celestial vines.
  5. Sprinkle with nebular herbs and spices, stellar salt, and black pepper to taste.
  6. Drizzle with nebulaic olive oil and let the cosmic soup simmer until the flavors meld together.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed to achieve the perfect cosmic balance.
  8. Ladle the steaming Galactic Harvest Vegetable Soup into bowls, garnishing with intergalactic herbs for an added touch of celestial delight.
  9. Embark on a cosmic culinary adventure as you savor the harmonious flavors of this extraordinary soup.
