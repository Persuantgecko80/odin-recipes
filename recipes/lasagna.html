Layers of Flavor and Comfort: Indulge in Homemade Lasagna Goodness!

Lasagna is a delicious and comforting dish that consists of layers of pasta, savory sauce, cheese, and often includes meat or vegetables. Here's a breakdown of the ingredients and steps to make a classic lasagna:

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles - approximately 12 sheets
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage - 1 pound (450g)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves - 2, minced
  • Tomato sauce - 2 cups
  • Canned crushed tomatoes - 1 cup
  • Italian seasoning - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper - ½ teaspoon
  • Ricotta cheese - 2 cups
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese - 2 cups
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - ½ cup
  • Fresh basil or parsley - for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles and set aside.
  3. Brown ground beef or Italian sausage with onion and garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper to make the sauce.
  5. Combine ricotta cheese, half of the mozzarella, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Layer meat sauce, noodles, and ricotta mixture in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat the layers, finishing with sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  9. Cover and bake until bubbly and cheese is melted.
  10. Remove foil and bake for a few more minutes to brown the cheese.
  11. Let it rest before serving.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
